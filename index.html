About Me

Hello, I'm Daniel! I'm a sophomore at the University of Minnesota Duluth studying Human Resource Management. When I graduate, I hope to get a full-time job in Human Resource Management. I enjoy connecting with people, building supportive communities, and continually learning how to help teams grow.

Passion for Theatre

I'm very passionate about theatre and audition for plays and musicals whenever they fit my schedule. Even if I can't perform in a show myself, I still enjoy watching others perform on stage. Theatre has taught me discipline, teamwork, and how to support those around me.

Love for Skiing

Cross-country skiing is one of my favorite winter activities! I'm better at classic skiing, but skate skiing is my favorite of the two. Whether I'm racing friends or exploring trails, skiing keeps me active, focused, and connected to the outdoors.
